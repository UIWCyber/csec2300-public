Architecture and Diagrams

0 to 4 Score / 4
Needs Improvement Satisfactory Excellent
Diagrams are missing, messy, or inaccurate. Standard diagrams show containers, networks, and trust boundaries, and they match the implementation. Professional topology plus data-flow diagrams that explain how the system actually works.

Technical Documentation

0 to 4 Score / 4
Needs Improvement Satisfactory Excellent
Vague documentation ("we installed the firewall"), with no screenshots or configurations. Explains the build steps covering the target controls, and includes screenshots and configurations. Runbook-style detail: someone else could rebuild the stack from the wiki alone, and it explains why each setting was chosen.

Wiki Structure and Navigation

0 to 2 Score / 2

One long page, or broken links. A sidebar and logical sections (Home, Architecture, Setup, Sprint Notes). Mirrors a professional open-source project: easy navigation and clean headings.
