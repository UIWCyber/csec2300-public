# Capstone Document Pack

**CSEC 2300 Foundations of Cyber Security** | Instructor: Dr. Gonzalo D Parra

The capstone is a semester-long team project: build, harden, and document a **secure local-AI stack** (Dockerized Ollama + Open WebUI + an AI agent) delivered through a security-gated CI pipeline, in a GitHub repository with a Project board and a Wiki final report. Full guidelines and grading weights are in the course outline (sections 5.1 and 5.2); all deadlines are posted on Canvas, and no late work is accepted.

**These are the files to upload to Canvas** (each is self-contained and printable):

| Document | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| [team-formation-guide.md](team-formation-guide.md) | How to form a team of 2 to 3 (no solo work), compare schedules before committing, split roles fairly, and what to do if a team falls apart. |
| [proposal-template.md](proposal-template.md) | The fill-in template teams submit for instructor approval: roster and availability, stack configuration, target controls mapped to CO1 through CO6 and SY0-701 domains, milestone plan, risks, and signatures. |
| [readiness-checklist.md](readiness-checklist.md) | The pre-presentation self-audit: CI green on HEAD, hardened containers, TLS, guarded agent, complete docs, board hygiene, and demo dry-run, each with a how-to-verify line. |
| [presentation-rubric.md](presentation-rubric.md) | The grading rubric for the final presentation: five weighted criteria with excellent, adequate, and missing descriptors, the every-member-speaks rule, and the missed-slot deduction. |
| [peer-feedback-form.md](peer-feedback-form.md) | The one-page form students complete during classmates' presentations for participation credit: one strength, one gap, one question, and clarity ratings. |

**Suggested posting order on Canvas:** team-formation-guide with the capstone announcement, proposal-template with the Capstone Proposal assignment, readiness-checklist around checkpoint 2, and presentation-rubric plus peer-feedback-form with the presentation schedule.
