Capstone Peer Feedback Form

CSEC 2300 Foundations of Cyber Security  |  Instructor: Dr. Gonzalo D Parra
Fill out one form per presenting team while they present. Submitting these forms counts toward your participation grade. Be specific and constructive: name what you saw, not the people. Feedback goes to the presenting team after grading.

1. One Strength

Something this team did well: a control done right, a sharp demo moment, an honest risk call. Point to a specific moment in the talk.

2. One Gap

Something missing, unclear, or that you would harden further. A gap is an observation, not an insult; the best gaps name a specific control or slide.

3. One Question

A question you would ask this team about their stack, their controls, or a risk they accepted (whether or not you get to ask it in Q&A).

4. Clarity Ratings

Choose one rating per row. 1 = unclear, 5 = crystal clear.

AspectRating (1 to 5)

5. One Idea to Steal (optional)

Something from this presentation you would use in your own work.
