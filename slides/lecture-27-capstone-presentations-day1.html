Learning Objectives

  2. Map implemented controls to course outcomes and SY0-701 domains (CO6).
  3. Explain mitigations for network, LLM, and agent attacks (CO2), crypto and access controls (CO3), and privacy and policy choices (CO4).

Rubric Detail

  • Architecture & working demo (25%): the stack runs; segmentation, TLS, and containers are evident.
  • Security controls & mapping (30%): controls named, classified, and mapped to a CO and a SY0-701 domain.
  • Attack & defense narrative (20%): a real attack shown and mitigated, with honest residual risk.
  • Governance artifacts (15%): risk register, AI-use policy, IR note, and clean Git and board history.
  • Delivery & teamwork (10%): every member speaks; clear, on time, and answers questions.
