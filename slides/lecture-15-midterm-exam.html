Opening the Room

  • On screen at the start: exam access code (revealed only when the window opens), Wi-Fi network name, "phones in bags, laptops only on the exam."
  • Verbal script: one attempt, roughly forty multiple-choice plus four short scenarios, closed notes, every question maps to a lecture objective.

During the Exam Window

  • Reveal the access code and start a visible countdown timer on the projector.
  • Circulate the room regularly; watch for second devices and screen-sharing.
  • Technical-failure protocol: move the student to a spare laptop, note the timestamp, and extend their timer by the time lost through Canvas moderation.
  • Early finishers submit, pack quietly, and may leave.

Closing the Window

Contingencies

  • Evacuation or alarm: note the time, then invalidate and reschedule per department policy.
  • Campus network outage: paper backup copies are in the bag; department scantron is the fallback.
  • Student illness mid-exam: submit as-is with a timestamp note and arrange a makeup per the syllabus.
