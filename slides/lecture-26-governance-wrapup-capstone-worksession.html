The Course-Wide Security Checklist (self-audit)

  • Handout: about twenty line items across M1 through M10, each tagged to the outcome or domain it evidences.

Common Gaps We Still See

  • Ollama port still published to the host; :latest tags instead of pinned digests; secrets in the compose environment instead of files.
  • No output validation on the agent; no human gate on a sensitive tool; an empty or stale Project board.
  • A README without a threat model; the wiki missing the risk register.
Watch outThese are the recurring point-losers from prior checkpoints. If any is RED for your team, fix it before you add anything new.

Presentation Format & Logistics

  • Presentation days: teams present in posted time slots (on Canvas); a live demo plus slides; every member speaks.
